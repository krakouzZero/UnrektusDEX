Two Types of Participants, Two Ways to Earn

1. Traders (REKT)

These are users who actually lost money on trades. Submit your losing trades and compete for the Prize Pool Traders.

  • The Traders’ prize pool is distributed among the top-ranked submissions at the end of each session.

2. Voters (VOTERS)

These are users who hold the $uREKT token and vote for their favorite submissions.

  • Earn your share of the Prize Pool Voters, which comes from the “bribes” placed by Traders.
  • Receive a portion of these bribes in proportion to the votes you cast.

How It Works – In Detail

  1. Step 1: Join the Ongoing Session
    Each session lasts 3 days (except the first one, which may be longer).
  2. Step 2: Choose Your Strategy
    • Trader (REKT): Submit losing trades, pay submission fees, and compete for votes.
    • Voter: Stake your $uREKT, cast votes, and earn bribe rewards.
  3. Step 3: Boosts & Strategy
    Boost your submission ranking or optimize votes to maximize rewards.
  4. Step 4: Reward Distribution
    Prize pools are allocated, and tokens are reclaimed at the session’s end.
